## IMPORTANT NOTES

**Privacy:** Never use photos that clearly identify a specific real child. Unsplash photos are fine — they are licensed for commercial and non-commercial use.

**Representation:** The brand guidelines explicitly say imagery must be inclusive. Look for photos that show families of different ethnicities and backgrounds. Unsplash has good diversity if you browse by topic rather than always taking the top result.

**What to avoid per the brand guidelines:**
- Beaming stock-photo families with perfect kitchens
- Medical or clinical imagery (stethoscopes, hospital settings)
- High-contrast or very busy images
- Exclusively white Western family representation

**File sizes:** Keep each image under 500KB for fast loading. Use tinypng.com (free) to compress them after downloading.

**Alternative to Unsplash:** Pexels (pexels.com) is also free and has similar quality. Pixabay is another option.

## QUICK DOWNLOAD METHOD ON UNSPLASH

1. Go to unsplash.com
2. Type the search term shown above
3. Look for images that match the description — candid, warm, real
4. Click the image → click the down arrow (↓) to download free
5. Rename the file to match the filename shown above
6. Drop it in your `images/` folder alongside your HTML files
7. Refresh the page in your browser — it will appear automatically
